About Us

Our journey started in 2015 when we saw potential in kids playing street football. We decided to form a team and start another journey. Back then, we had no jerseys — only our coach who gave us hope and told us never to give up despite the struggles of wanting to become the best one day.

We stick to one motto: #ONETEAMONESPIRIT. Today, we have grown far from where we began. By God’s grace, one of our players is set to transfer to the KPL and play for GOR MAHIA FC. Another achievement in 2025 is that our Under 13 and Under 15 teams are competing in the FKF league.

Merchandise and kits are also another achievement coming soon — so stay tuned. We love you, our fans! #ONETEAMONESPIRIT
